Project S.U.N. (Sensory Ultrasonic Navigation)

An engineering project which utilizes ultrasonic sensors to aid the visually impaired in navigation. Two sets of werables, a glove and shoulder component, were created to house the ultrasonic sensors and vibrational and pieze motors that gave tactile and auditory feedback to the user. This project won "Best in Fair" in a regional science fair and went on to participate in the Intel International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF).

Teammates: Zachary Guo & Jeremy Tan
